Hridayam

                                       "Hridayam" is a 2022 Malayalam coming-of-age romantic drama film written and directed by Vineeth Sreenivasan, starring Pranav Mohanlal, Kalyani Priyadarshan, and Darshana Rajendran. The film follows the life of Arun Neelakandan, from his college days in Chennai to his journey through love, heartbreak, and personal growth. With its nostalgic portrayal of campus life, relatable characters, and Hesham Abdul Wahab's soulful music, "Hridayam" struck a chord with audiences, becoming a critical and commercial success. The film's emotional core, strong performances, and Vineeth Sreenivasan's signature style of storytelling made it a memorable addition to Malayalam cinema, resonating with viewers for its honest depiction of life's ups and downs.
