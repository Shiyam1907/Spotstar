M.S.Dhoni - The Untold Story

                                       "M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story" is a biographical sports film released in 2016, directed by Neeraj Pandey, that chronicles the life and career of former Indian cricket captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni. The film stars Sushant Singh Rajput in the titular role and explores Dhoni's journey from his humble beginnings in Ranchi to becoming one of the most successful captains in cricket history. It highlights key moments in his life, including his struggles, personal relationships, and the pivotal moments that shaped his career, such as his iconic performances in the 2007 T20 World Cup and the 2011 ODI World Cup. The film received positive reviews for its engaging storytelling and Rajput's portrayal of Dhoni, and it resonated with fans for its inspirational message about perseverance and following one's dreams. "M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story" not only celebrates Dhoni's achievements but also provides insight into the man behind the cricketing legend, making it a significant addition to sports cinema in India.
