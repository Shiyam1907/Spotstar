Neru

                                       "Neru" is a 2023 Malayalam courtroom drama series directed by Jeethu Joseph, currently streaming on Disney+ Hotstar. The series revolves around Sara, a blind artist who becomes a victim of a heinous crime and her quest for justice against powerful forces seeking to undermine her case. Starring Mohanlal as the lawyer Vijayamohan and Anaswara Rajan as Sara, the series has been praised for its compelling narrative, strong performances, and exploration of themes such as justice and resilience in the face of adversity. "Neru" effectively combines suspense with emotional depth, making it a significant addition to the genre of legal dramas in Malayalam streaming content. The series has received positive reviews for its engaging storytelling and the way it highlights the challenges faced by a blind victim in navigating the Indian legal system.
