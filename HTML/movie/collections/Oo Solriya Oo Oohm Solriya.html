Oo Solriya Oo Oohm Solriya

                                       Oo Solriya Oo Oohm Solriya (ஊ சொல்றியா ஓஓஓம் சொல்றியா) is an 2022 Indian-Tamil language quiz reality television show that airs on Star Vijay and streamed on Disney+ Hotstar. The shows has two seasons. Ma Ka Pa Anand and Priyanka Deshpande has continued as a host for two seasons.It is a Yes or No Based quiz show, where the Tamil Television celebrities enter to win a huge cash prize by using their intelligence.[6] The first season was premiered on 4 September 2022 and ended with 26 Episode from 19 March 2023. The second season premiered on 19 October 2023.
