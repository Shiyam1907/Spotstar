Cooku with Comali

                                       "Cooku with Comali" is a popular Tamil-language cooking competition and comedy show that premiered on Star Vijay on November 16, 2019. The show pairs amateur cooks with comedians, known as "comalis," who add a humorous twist to the cooking tasks, creating a unique blend of culinary challenges and entertainment. Each week, contestants face various cooking tasks judged by renowned chefs, while the comalis contribute to the comedic elements of the show, making it a stress-buster for viewers. The show's engaging format and the chemistry between participants have led to its immense popularity, resulting in multiple successful seasons and a dedicated fan base. "Cooku with Comali" has been praised for its light-hearted approach, making it a refreshing addition to the reality TV landscape in Tamil Nadu, and continues to stream on Disney+ Hotstar.
