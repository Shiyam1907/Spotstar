Mr & Mrs Chinnathirai

                                       "Mr & Mrs Chinnathirai" is a popular Tamil reality show that features married couples from the Tamil television industry showcasing their talents and skills. Premiered in 2019 on Star Vijay and streamed on Disney+ Hotstar, the show has successfully completed four seasons, with the fifth season currently airing. Each season showcases a new set of couples competing in various challenges to avoid elimination and become the winners. The show is hosted by Ma Ka Pa Anand and Aranthangi Nisha, while Devadarshini and Gopinath Chandran serve as the judges. "Mr & Mrs Chinnathirai" has gained immense popularity for its entertaining format, talented contestants, and the unique opportunity it provides for real-life couples to bond and grow together through the challenges presented on the show.
