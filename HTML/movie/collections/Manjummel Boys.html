Manjummel Boys

                                       "Manjummel Boys" is a Malayalam survival thriller film directed by Chidambaram, inspired by a true incident that occurred in the Guna Caves of Kodaikanal. The film follows a group of friends from Manjummel who embark on a trip that takes a perilous turn when one of them falls into a deep chasm, leading to a desperate rescue mission. The narrative explores themes of camaraderie, resilience, and the challenges faced by the boys as they confront the dangers of their adventurous spirit. Featuring a talented cast including Soubin Shahir and Sreenath Bhasi, the film has been praised for its engaging storytelling, character development, and the effective portrayal of suspense and emotion during critical moments. With a successful box office run, "Manjummel Boys" has resonated with audiences for its blend of humor and tension, solidifying its place in the genre of survival films in Malayalam cinema.
