SKY, India's New T20I
Captain

                                       Suryakumar Yadav has been appointed as the new captain of the Indian T20 cricket team for the upcoming series against Sri Lanka, marking a significant transition in leadership following Rohit Sharma's tenure. This decision comes as part of a broader strategy by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) to infuse fresh energy into the team, especially with the T20 World Cup approaching. Suryakumar, known for his explosive batting and innovative stroke play, aims to lead the team with a focus on aggressive gameplay and resilience. His appointment reflects the selectors' confidence in his abilities to guide the team through the challenges ahead, as they prepare for three T20 matches in Pallekele starting July 27, 2024.
