Selfie with Bajrangi

                                       "Selfie with Bajrangi" is a popular Indian animated series that follows the comedic adventures of a character named Ankush and his interactions with Bajrangi, a representation of the beloved character from the film "Bajrangi Bhaijaan." The show, which is available on platforms like Prime Video, revolves around Ankush's daily challenges and how he overcomes them with the help of Bajrangi, often leading to humorous situations. The series is designed to entertain children and families, combining elements of comedy with life lessons, and has garnered a following for its engaging storytelling and relatable characters. Each episode features different scenarios that highlight friendship, problem-solving, and the importance of good values, making it a delightful watch for audiences of all ages.
