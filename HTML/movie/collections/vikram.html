VIKRAM

                                       "Vikram" is a 2022 Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, featuring Kamal Haasan in the titular role alongside Vijay Sethupathi and Fahadh Faasil. The film serves as a spiritual successor to the 1986 film of the same name and is set within the Lokesh Cinematic Universe. The plot follows Vikram, a former commander of a black-ops team, as he investigates a series of murders linked to a mysterious gang of masked men and a powerful drug lord named Sandhanam. The film is noted for its intense action sequences, engaging storyline, and strong performances, particularly by Haasan and Sethupathi.
