Jodi Are U Ready

                                       Jodi Are U Ready is a 2024 Indian Tamil-language reality television dance competition show and is the eleventh part of Jodi No.1. Rio Raj and Angelin were as hosts of the show which was judged by Actress Meena, Choreographer Sandy Master and Sridevi Vijaykumar are the judges of the show.It premiered on January 20, 2024, and aired on Star Vijay every Saturday and Sunday.[2] It is also available for streaming on Disney+ Hotstar.The show launched on 20 January and held its finale episode on 28 April 2024. The show was won by duo Dhanush and Justina.While other duo Vignesh and Raveena Daha emerged as the runner up of the show. While Prajwal and Ramya emerged as third place. Actresses Nikki Galrani and Sayyeshaa also appeared as a special guest and judge during the grand finale.
