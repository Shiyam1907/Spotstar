Jonita Gandhi

"Jonita Gandhi is a renowned Canadian playback singer of Indian descent who has made significant contributions to Indian cinema, recording songs predominantly in Hindi, Tamil, and Telugu languages, with a few in Punjabi, Marathi, Gujarati, Bengali, Kannada, and Malayalam. Her singing career began with the title song from the film "Chennai Express" in 2013, and she has since collaborated with acclaimed music directors like A.R. Rahman and Pritam. Gandhi is known for her versatility, vocal range, and soulful renditions, with some of her most acclaimed songs being "The Breakup Song," "Mental Manadhil," "Chellama," and "Arabic Kuthu." She has a strong presence on YouTube, where her covers of popular Hindi film songs have gained millions of views, and in 2023, she became the first female artist to grace the cover of Billboard Canada, paving the way for more female artists in the industry".
