Laapataa Ladies

"Laapataa Ladies is a Hindi film directed by Kiran Rao and produced by Aamir Khan, Kiran Rao, and Jyoti Deshpande. The movie features a star-studded cast including Nitanshi Goel, Pratibha Ranta, Sparsh Shrivastava, Ravi Kishan, and Chhaya Kadam. The film's soundtrack is composed by Ram Sampath, with songs like "Beda Paar" and "Sajni" being notable highlights. "Beda Paar" is sung by Sona Mohapatra, while "Sajni" is sung by Arijit Singh and remixed by DJ Basque. The lyrics for both songs are written by Prashant Pandey".
