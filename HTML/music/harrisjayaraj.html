Harris Jayaraj

"Harris Jayaraj is a renowned Indian music composer, producer, and songwriter, born on January 8, 1975, in Chennai. He began his music career as a guitarist at age 12 and later learned keyboard and synthesizers. Jayaraj made his debut as a music director with the 2001 film "Minnale," which was a major success. He has since composed music for over 100 films in Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi, earning numerous awards including six Filmfare Awards South, six Mirchi Music Awards, and four Tamil Nadu State Film Awards. He is known for his Western-influenced orchestration and has worked with various artists, including A. R. Rahman and Gautham Menon. Jayaraj has also been recognized with an honorary doctorate from Dr. MGR Educational and Research Institute and the Kalaimamani Award from the Government of Tamil Nadu"
