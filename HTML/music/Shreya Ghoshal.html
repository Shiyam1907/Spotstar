Shreya Ghoshal

"Shreya Ghoshal is one of the most prolific and popular playback singers in India, known for her wide vocal range, versatility, and remarkable vocal expressions. Born in 1984 in West Bengal, Ghoshal began learning music at the age of four and started her formal training in classical music at six. She gained recognition after winning the television singing reality show Sa Re Ga Ma at the age of sixteen. Since then, Ghoshal has recorded songs for films and albums in various Indian and foreign languages, receiving numerous accolades including five National Film Awards, four Kerala State Film Awards, two BFJA Awards, seven Filmfare Awards, and ten Filmfare Awards South. Widely regarded as one of the greatest vocalists of the Indian subcontinent, Ghoshal has been featured in the Forbes list of the top 100 celebrities from India five times and had her wax figure displayed at the Madame Tussauds Museum in Delhi in 2017, becoming the first Indian singer to be honored in this way".
