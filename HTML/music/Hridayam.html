Hridayam

"Hridayam, the highly anticipated Malayalam film directed by Vineeth Sreenivasan, features a diverse and captivating soundtrack composed by Hesham Abdul Wahab. The album boasts an impressive 15 songs that seamlessly blend traditional Indian classical elements with contemporary pop, rock, and Middle Eastern influences. From the soulful "Darshana" featuring Hesham and Darshana Rajendran, to the upbeat "Pottu Thotta Pournami" and the haunting "Nagumo" based on a Tyagaraja kriti, the Hridayam soundtrack showcases Hesham's versatility as a composer. The album also includes collaborations with renowned artists like K.S. Chithra, Prithviraj Sukumaran, and Unni Menon, further enhancing the musical richness of this cinematic experience. With its innovative arrangements, emotive lyrics, and diverse musical styles, the Hridayam soundtrack has been widely acclaimed for its ability to captivate listeners and seamlessly complement the film's narrative".
